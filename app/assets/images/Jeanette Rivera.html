Hello, I'm Jeanette

A web developer & production artist

My Story

I'm a full stack web developer & production artist based in New York City. I have over 5 years of experience in print and digital production and have recently expanded my horizons and ventured into the world of web development. Check out my work and drop me a line!


My Work

Meal123 is a meal planning app built with Ruby on Rails. It utilizes ActiveRecord and Devise.

Green Thumb Nursery was developed in collaboration with Sarah Niemiec. It demonstrates our understanding of routing within Sinatra as well as showcases our UI/UX sensibilities. It utilizes SendGrid for the contact form and JavaScript for the carousel and modals.

Brainfloss is a fun and whimsical app built with Ruby on Rails that provides users with random facts and trivia generated from 3 APIs and a hardcoded database.
